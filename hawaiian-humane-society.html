The Problem on Oahu

Thousands of animals need help. The work never stops.

Oahu is a beautiful island. But every week, 400 to 600 animals arrive at Hawaiian Humane needing medical care, shelter, and a second chance. Behind every statistic is a dog who was lost, a litter of kittens with no mother, or a family that needed help keeping their pet during a hard season. Without consistent funding, programs get cut. Animals wait longer. Families fall through the cracks.

Hundreds of animals arrive at Hawaiian Humane every single week, each needing food, shelter, and veterinary care before they can find a home.

One-time donations help, but they create uncertainty. Monthly donors are what let the team plan, hire, and show up every single day without interruption.

Without spay/neuter access for low-income families, pet overpopulation compounds year after year. Your monthly gift funds the surgeries that break that cycle.

Why Hawaiian Humane

Oahu's most trusted animal welfare organization

Founded in 1883 by 350 concerned citizens and King Kalakaua himself, Hawaiian Humane is one of the oldest humane societies in the nation. We are not a chapter of any national group. Every dollar you give stays in Hawaii, helping the animals and people of Oahu. Today, 30+ programs and services cover the full range of animal welfare needs: adoptions, foster care, spay/neuter, cruelty investigations, pet food assistance, education, and disaster response.

Your Questions, Answered

We want you to feel completely confident

How is my monthly donation used? +
Your monthly gift directly funds the daily operations of Hawaiian Humane: veterinary care for shelter animals, food and housing, spay/neuter surgeries, humane investigations, and community programs like the Pet Food Bank. We maintain full financial transparency and are recognized by Charity Navigator and GuideStar.
Can I cancel or change my monthly gift at any time? +
Yes, absolutely. You can pause, reduce, increase, or cancel your monthly gift at any time by contacting us at Giving@HawaiianHumane.org or calling (808) 356-2200. There are no penalties and no guilt trips. We just hope you stay.
Is my donation tax-deductible? +
Yes. Hawaiian Humane Society is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN 99-0073490). All donations are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. You will automatically receive a receipt for every monthly gift for your records.
Does Hawaiian Humane operate as part of any national organization? +
No. We are a fully independent nonprofit. We are not affiliated with the ASPCA, HSUS, or any other national group. Every single dollar you donate stays in Hawaii and benefits the animals and people of Oahu directly.
Will I see how my monthly gift is making a difference? +
Absolutely. As an Aloha 'Ohana member you will receive monthly impact emails with real stories from the shelter, updates on animals your gift has helped, and behind-the-scenes content from our team. Higher tiers also receive personal photos and exclusive updates.
What happens if I miss a payment? +
If a payment fails, our team will reach out gently to help resolve the issue. We never assume anything and always give donors the benefit of the doubt. Life happens, and we are grateful for any support you can give.
Can I give a monthly gift in honor of someone? +
Yes. We love tribute and memorial gifts. You can set up a recurring tribute gift at hawaiianhumane.salsalabs.org/tributegifts and we will notify the recipient or their family of your thoughtful ongoing generosity.
How does Hawaiian Humane handle the animals it receives? +
We are an open admission shelter, which means we accept animals regardless of age, health, or behavior. Every animal receives a physical evaluation, spay/neuter surgery, microchipping, vaccinations, and treatment before being placed for adoption. We have 30+ programs to find the right outcome for every animal.
What other ways can I help beyond donating? +
Lots of ways. You can foster an animal through our Couch Crashers or Lap Crashers programs, volunteer at one of our two campuses, donate pet food and supplies, advocate for stronger animal welfare laws, or shop our Amazon Wish List. Every action matters.
Does my employer match charitable donations? +
Many employers offer matching gift programs that could double or triple your monthly impact. Check with your HR department or search your company's matching gift portal. If you need our EIN or documentation, contact us at Giving@HawaiianHumane.org.
